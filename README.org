* Summary

Proof of concept to join tables on fuzzy strings with
~difflib.get_close_matches~ in Python 3. For example

| Id1 | Name1 |
|-----+-------|
| a1  | Foo1  |
| b1  | Bar1  |

joins with

| Id2 | Name2 |
|-----+-------|
| a2  | Bar2  |
| b2  | Foo2  |

to

| Id1 | Name1 | Matched2-zENjn | Id2 | Name2 |
|-----+-------+----------------+-----+-------|
| a1  | Foo1  | Foo2           | b2  | Foo2  |
| b1  | Bar1  | Bar2           | a2  | Bar2  |
